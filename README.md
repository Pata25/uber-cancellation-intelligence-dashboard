## Business Problem

When does a rider become likely to cancel an Uber ride?

This project analyses ride-level data to identify the operational factors driving cancellations and revenue loss.

## Key Findings

* 35.33% overall cancellation rate
* $1,192 estimated revenue lost
* Surge pricing is a stronger cancellation driver than ETA
* High ETA + High Surge rides cancel 80.56% of the time
* Miami has the highest cancellation rate (44%)
* New York generates the highest revenue loss
